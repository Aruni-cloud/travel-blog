My Travel Stories

Kyoto,Japan

Kyoto is a serene blend of ancient temples, peaceful gardens, and traditional tea houses nestled among forested hills.

Highlights:

  • Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine
  • Explore Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • Enjoy a traditional kaiseki meal

Paris, France

The City of Light dazzles with its iconic landmarks, romantic ambiance, and world-class cuisine.

Highlights:

  • Climb the Eiffel Tower
  • Stroll along the Seine River
  • Visit the Louvre Museum

Ella, Sri Lanka

Ella is a lush hill town surrounded by tea plantations, waterfalls, and breathtaking mountain views.

Highlights:

  • Hike to Ella Rock
  • Visit Nine Arches Bridge
  • Relax at Ravana Falls
